Our privacy policy

This is the privacy policy for Justify.

The data we save

We save the following data about your usage:

  • Your name
  • Your ID number
  • Your justification requests
  • A unique authentication token

What the saved data lets us do

With your saved data, we can authenticate your login and forward your approval requests to the mashov system.

What the data doesn't let us do

Your data does not let us log into your mashov or see any confidential data not stored.

The data we don't save

We don't save:

  • Your mashov login details - the username and password
  • Your absences
